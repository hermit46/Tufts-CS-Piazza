What's this website for?

Hey there, We're a couple of juniors @ Tufts studying Computer Science! The CS Jobs & Career Opportunity Piazza (or CS 199, if you're old school) has been a great avenue for exploring volunteer, internship & new grad positions, along with solid career advice from Professor Ming Chow & seniors/alums. We decided that it needed a new paint job & some reorganization, so that Jumbos can search here for the opportunities that matter most to them.

We hope that the Piazza will continue to serve as a repository of career advice, while this website becomes a list of open opportunities!
